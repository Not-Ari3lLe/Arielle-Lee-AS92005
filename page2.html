Environmental issues of deforestation

Deforestation can cause multiple problems like climate change, also contributing to global warming.

This also affects the water cycle, damaging natural habitats (habitat loss to animals that lives in the forest) as well with soil erosion.

Deforestation can cause air pollution to become worse, also increases the risk of pandemics.


Deforestation will not only affect the environment and habitat loss, but this can negatively impact on our daily lives.
We depend on these natural resources, we need trees to live.
