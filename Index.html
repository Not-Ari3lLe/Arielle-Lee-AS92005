Deforestation NZ

Deforestation happens when people cut down or clear trees in forests over a large area.
(this site contains information about deforestation and more!)

Deforestation is a big issue because it harms the environment and animals that are adapted in the forest.

About us: our mission is to provide safe and effective strategies to stop deforestation,
also spreading the word kaitiakitanga around us to encourage everyone that are interested to step in.
