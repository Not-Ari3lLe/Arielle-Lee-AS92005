Deforestation NZ

Deforestation happens when people cut down or clear trees in forests.
(this site contains information about deforestation and more!)

Deforestation is a big issue because it harms the environment and animals that are adapted in the forest.

About us: our mission is to provide safe and effective strategies to stop deforestation. we encourage everyone to spread kaitiakitanga around us.
