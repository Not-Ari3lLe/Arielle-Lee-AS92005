Kaitiakitanga: Guardianship and Protection

Kaitiakitanga is a Maori term that means Guardianship and protection.
(How we manage the environment, our responsibility and sustainability of the environment)

We encourage young adults to contribute and spread the word of Kaitiakitanga


link: Sustainable Forestry: a 2-minute overview.
link: Sustainable Forestry: Being Guardians of the Land in New Zealand .

A few ways we can do to contribute to deforestation are:

- Avoid buying products from companies who are responsible for deforestation, use reusable resources more often.


- Spreading Information about Deforestation to others and the potential consequences acting towards deforestation


- Joining / Engaging in sustainable programs

link: Greenpeace Volunteering platform.

By doing this, we are spreading the word of Kaitiakitanga. Helping the Environment by contributing to the loss of our forests.
We can make the world a better place, We strongly encourage people to contribute to deforestation.
